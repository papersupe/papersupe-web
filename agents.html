Agent Circle

Agent Circle is PaperSupe’s experimental environment for a single agentic AI system designed to reason over research, connect ideas across papers, and assist advanced research workflows.

Research Reading

The agent reads academic papers, extracts core arguments, and builds conceptual summaries beyond surface-level abstracts.

Concept Mapping

The agent links ideas across domains—connecting models, assumptions, methods, and theoretical frameworks.

Workflow Assistance

The agent supports literature review, hypothesis formation, and iterative research exploration.

Agent Status

Status: Experimental · Reading-Only

The agent can analyze and reason over research papers, but does not yet accept live queries or execute actions.
